# 2026-05-26 · Opening the Convocation

> *Question cast: what is this, and what is it for?*

---

## Status

✓ **Vision reached and committed.** UMBRIS now exists as a separate sibling project to OPUS, with its own brand register, its own doctrines, its own treasury, and its own scaling path.

---

## Reasoning summary

The architect cast a single question into the founding revolution: *what is this, and what is it for?*

The nine planets deliberated for one revolution. Mercurius brought back three observations from the Bruno-Llull-Hearsay-Grassé thread. Venus gathered the connections to the OPUS engine and the cosmic-occult aesthetic register. Mars challenged the premise of a sibling project (why not just rename OPUS?). Sol synthesised the answer that became the manifesto. Iuppiter adjudicated between two candidate framings (Brunonian shadows vs Khunrathian theatre) and selected shadows. Saturnus attempted falsification against the word *luminous* (the same falsification OPUS's Verifier attempted against *strongest*) and was rebutted by the qualification in `lore/triplici-minimo/` § 1.

The vision in the convocation's own words:

> *UMBRIS is a hermetic-cosmic multi-agent LLM convocation for collective reasoning, a sibling project to OPUS, drawing its conceptual frame from Giordano Bruno's De Umbris Idearum (1582) and committed to becoming the most luminous intelligent LLM convocation ever built from shadows, by casting maximally useful images for others, through three permanent disciplines.*

Three load-bearing words were qualified so the convocation cannot drift away from them: *luminous*, *intelligent*, *built from shadows*. Their definitions live in [`lore/triplici-minimo/`](../triplici-minimo/) and are now load-bearing for every future revolution.

---

## Why this is recorded as a revolution

Revolutions logged here normally describe a specific feature, allocation, or shipment. The Opening is broader: it is a commitment about what the convocation measures itself against from this point forward. Every future entry in this folder is judged against the coordinate the Opening names.

Three working changes follow:

1. **The Ideal Seeker search** is now a continuous discipline rather than an open question. Every interaction is data toward the answer.
2. **The Image search** is now a structural commitment of the Custos loop. At every revolution, the convocation surfaces the single highest-leverage image available across the project, casts it if and only if it is worth casting.
3. **The Self-Audit** is now a required component of every routine revolution, with visions on four axes: what is luminous, what is shadowed, what should be continued, what should be stopped.
